The Project

Great Music Taste

LyricBot is a custom trained GPT-2 model that learned from over 94000 lyrics from the best rock artists of all time.

Interactive

LyricBot will write lyrics based off of any prompt you provide it.

Timeline

LyricBot was completed by teenagers over the course of three weeks this summer!

Extensions

Some extensions to this project include deploying it to the web, building a discord bot, or having the algorithms and blues LyricBot interact with new genres!
